# bike_sharing_dataset
Data analysis of bike rental demand

## Overview
This project analyzes bike rental demand to identify when and under what conditions the service is most used. The goal is to understand customer behavior and provide actionable business recommendations.

## Dataset
- Source: Kaggle Bike Sharing Dataset  
- Includes data on:
  - Date and time
  - Weather conditions
  - Temperature (feels like)
  - Humidity and windspeed
  - User types (casual and registered)

## Tools Used
- Excel (data cleaning, transformation, pivot tables, analysis)

## Key Insights

- Demand peaks during summer and early fall, especially at the end of the work week (Thursday–Friday).
- The optimal perceived temperature range is 25–35°C, where both total demand and casual user activity are highest.
- Weather has a strong impact on demand:
  - Sunny days generate significantly more usage than cloudy or rainy days.
  - Rainy conditions drastically reduce casual user activity.
- Casual users are highly sensitive to weather and external conditions, while registered users show more stable, routine behavior.
- Demand is lowest at the beginning of the week and during winter months.
- Extreme conditions (very high/low temperatures, strong wind, high humidity) negatively affect demand.
- Humidity and windspeed have a limited impact under normal conditions but reduce demand significantly at extreme levels.

## Business Recommendations

- Focus customer acquisition efforts on weekends and holidays, when casual user activity is highest.
- Introduce targeted promotions or discounts to convert casual users into registered customers.
- Increase bike availability and maintenance capacity during peak demand periods (summer, end of week, good weather).
- Improve utilization during low-demand days (Monday–Tuesday) through targeted promotions.
- Align marketing campaigns with favorable weather conditions to maximize engagement.
- Consider expansion in regions with stable and moderate weather for more consistent demand.

## Visualizations
The project includes multiple charts and dashboards showing demand patterns based on time, weather, and customer type.

## Conclusion
Bike usage is strongly driven by a combination of weather conditions, temperature, and day type. Understanding these factors allows the business to optimize operations, improve customer acquisition, and increase overall efficiency.
